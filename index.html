How to read this page: Booked P&L is the official score — recomputed by the cloud engine after every closed 4-hour bar, including fees and funding. Live P&L is the extra unrealized move of currently open positions inside the bar that is still running, marked at Binance prices refreshed every 30 s; it becomes booked when the bar closes. The engine trades 5 coins; a coin with no position is a decision, not a gap — it means no volume-confirmed signal and no 3/3 bull regime right now.

Engine: GitHub Actions, every 4h at UTC bar close (07:07 / 11:07 / 15:07 / 19:07 / 23:07 / 03:07 BKK) — closing your laptop stops nothing. Data file dash.json is refreshed each cycle; live prices come from Binance’s public mirror. Expectation band: Sharpe ~1.0 · CAGR 30–45%/yr · maxDD > −35%. P&L includes maker fees + funding (CI runs on the spot mirror book funding as 0 until the perp API is reachable — the source label above says which).
